🔑 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to create a visually compelling and interactive dashboard to help businesses answer questions such as:

Which regions and categories are most profitable?

How do discounts impact overall profit margins?

What are the sales and shipping trends over time?

Which customer segments contribute most to revenue?

📊 Dashboard Features

KPI Cards: Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity

Category & Sub-Category Breakdown: Identify top-performing product categories

Regional Performance: Map visualization for State/Region insights

Time-Series Trends: Sales and profit trends by order date

Discount vs Profit Analysis: Understand impact of discounts

Interactive Filters: Slicers for Region, Segment, and Category

🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

Power BI – Data modeling, DAX measures, and dashboard design

Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

Data Analysis – Extracting meaningful insights from business data

🚀 How to Use

Download or clone this repository.

Open Superstore_Sales_Analysis.pbix in Power BI Desktop.

Interact with the dashboard by applying filters and exploring visuals.

💡 Key Insights

Identified top-performing regions and categories.

Highlighted profit-loss drivers influenced by discounts.

Discovered seasonal sales patterns and shipping timelines.
